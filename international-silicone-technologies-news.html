What's New at Intersil

Calendered Silicone Compounds

Intersil has the capability of calendering compounds. We can calender from a minimal 0.008 inch in thickness to a width of up to 56 inches. Do you need fabric reinforced calendered silicone? We make it. Available specs include:

  • AMS-3320
  • MIL-C-85052
  • BPS-C-1-63
  • And many others

Molded Silicone Sheets Now Available

Solid, sponge, fluorosilicone and fiberglass reinforced silicone sheets are now available in many standard sizes in domestic and foreign specs. Thicknesses range from .032'' to .500''.
